Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the app counting my reps?

Make sure your full body is visible in the camera frame and you're in a well-lit area. The camera needs a clear, unobstructed view to detect your movement accurately. Try stepping back so your whole body is in frame.

Which exercises are supported?

Rep AI currently supports push-ups. More exercises are being added in future updates.

Is my camera footage stored or shared?

No. Your camera feed is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I reset my workout history?

Go to the Settings screen inside the app and tap "Clear History" to remove all saved data.

The app isn't detecting my poses correctly

Ensure you have good lighting and a plain background if possible. Wear fitted clothing so your body outline is clearly visible to the camera.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened. We aim to fix reported bugs in the next update.
